Our Team

  • Our group was founded in 2018 by Mike, hard-wearing father-of-4 and Julia, mother of 3. The difficulties they encountered raising teenage children inspired them to reach out to other parents, share and eventually embrace these problems and help other parents overcome trying situations and problems.

  • They have become qualified therapists since. The group has been growing ever since, and has been involving 2 mental health professionals as well, Brian and Karina.

  • Brian brings 10 years clinical experience as a Counsellor & Psychotherapist for Children and Teenagers.

  • Karina holds a Masters Degree in Health Promotion and a BA (Hons) Degree in Social Care. She worked in the area of homelessness before moving into youth work to operate an internationally recognised youth mentoring programme.

Benefits

Group therapy can help parents work toward and meet many types of goals. Sometimes, the therapist might suggest group therapy over other forms of treatment. This could be because it is a good fit for that individual or better for treating their concern. Therapy groups can help parents whose problems it difficult to get through daily life. Others may not have pressing concerns but still wish for support. They can also benefit from group therapy.
