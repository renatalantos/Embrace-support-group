Welcome

Parenting teenagers can be challenging and many parents find it hard to adapt to changes in their child's behaviour as they grow.


We are a community of experienced parents and fully qualified mental health professionals volunteering to run a therapy support group to provide a high quality, safe, anonymous and confidential space for parents with teenage children to gain support, for whatever you are going through.


With us, you can relax, relate, share and realize that you are not on your own! Everything you share in the meeting about yourself and your family will be treated with the utmost confidentiality. You can stay anonymous if you wish.

Vision

We want to create a world where the hardships of parenting teenagers are acknowledged, accepted and talked about and where every parent in distress will feel confident to openly access our support group, based on a communal understanding and willingness to support each other.
