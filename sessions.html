Parent: “Have a nice day!”
Teenager: “Don’t tell me how to live my life!”

How It Happens

Parenting teenagers can be challenging and many parents find it hard to adapt to changes in their child's behaviour as they grow.
We are a community of experienced parents and fully qualified mental health professionals volunteering / charity to run a therapy support group to provide a high quality, safe, anonymous and confidential space for parents with teenage children to gain support, for whatever you are going through.
With us, you can relax, relate, share and realize that you are not on your own!
